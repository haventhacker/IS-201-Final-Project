Top Three Favorite Flowers

  1. Peonies


    • Known for their large, fragrant blooms
    • Available in a range of colors like pink, red, and white
    • Great for weddings and floral arrangements

  2. Tulips


    • Symbol of perfect love and elegance
    • Available in many colors including yellow, pink, red, and white
    • Bloom in early spring, making them a popular spring flower

  3. Dahlias


    • Known for their vibrant, intricate petals
    • Come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors
    • Perfect for adding a dramatic flair to any garden or bouquet
